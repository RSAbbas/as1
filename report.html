Site Report

Before coding my portfolio website, I went on GitHub and YouTube and searched for inspirations. After some time, I came across this tutorial here and started working on it.

As I began the process of creating my portfolio website, I searched YouTube and GitHub for ideas and direction among the multitude of guides and tutorials available. Following careful research, I came across a tutorial that matched my idea and gave me the motivation to start my project.

Selecting a colour scheme turned out to be a crucial choice; after many iterations, a pleasing combination of blue and black was created, painstakingly designed to capture the spirit of my style and go well with the pictures on the website, including the clothes that are featured there.

I searched Google for contemporary typographies and browsed through font selections, trying to find the ideal combination to capture the spirit of my story. Following extensive testing, a typeface that avoided excessively elaborate designs was determined to be the pinnacle of sophistication.

The seamless incorporation of animations and transitions is evidence of the intuitive nature of the creative process. Every component blended together beautifully, giving the website a dynamic and attractive feel that captivated users and led them through my digital story with ease.
